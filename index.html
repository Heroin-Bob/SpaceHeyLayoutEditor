Would you consider auto-update while editing?

Nope. I kind of hate auto-update. What ends up happening is you, at some point, add something like audio or an animation or whatever and then every time the screen updates that sound/animation/etc. restarts. Super annoying. Not going to do that.

Why does something I put on my page not display the same on SpaceHey?

In the roadmap you'll see that there is a "Match HTML/CSS/JS filters used on SpaceHey native" under the Considered column. There are some things you're simply not allowed to do on SpaceHey that I do not filter at this point in time. If your HTML/CSS/embed violates that rule then it will not work properly. There's really nothing I can do about that without adding the same filter to this page, which is very far down the list of things I would consider at this time. I also do not have access to that information so it may not be possible with 100% accuracy.
